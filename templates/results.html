How Accurate is your RFTM?

Unfortunately, the RFTM system is currently not very accurate. Your RFTM prediction is made by a model trained on only approximately 50 survey responses. The testing accuracy has been relatively low, at around 65%. Although this would be about 10 times more accurate than purely guessing your RFTM, it is very likely to make mistakes.

Furthr, even if the accuracy for your particular prediction was 100%, it may still not be a good characterization of your true investment-ality. This is because the training data was pre-processed such that only 2 possibilities of 4 attributes (R, F, T & M) were predicted by 8 demographic features. Your age, gender, work experience, etc. may not necessarily be the best predictors to say anything about your investment-ality, especially when they are ran through a K-Nearest-Neighbors model based on a limited sample size. For example, your nearest neighbors may be very different from you, because the distance between each neighbor may be very large (a sparse sample). For more information on KNN models, please feel free to visit this website by IBM.

Even if they are good predictors, the interpretation of RFTM categories may still not be a good representation of your investment-ality, e.g., overgeneralizing or not comprehensive enough.

Therefore, please don't be upset if you disagree with the RFTM explanation above! I recognize the limitations of my attempt, and will work on improving it. Thank you for participating!
